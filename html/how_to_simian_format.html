Simian Format Direction

  1. In Simian, go to File and click “New Program Log”
  2. Click “Event Builder”
  3. Drag over songs you’d like to play
    1. Search with the binoculars button
    2. If you’d like Simian to keep playing the next song, select the song you’d like to have Simian keep playing after and hit the “+” button. Also “+” to remove it.
    3. To remove a song, select it, right click and hit “cut”
  4. When you’re finished, go to File and click “Generic Log 01” or “Generic Log 02”
