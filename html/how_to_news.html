How–To: News at :55

  • All DJs between 7am & 5pm Monday– riday must read the news every :55
  • The USA News Network PDF can be found under the “News at :55” Bookmark
  • The first DJ of the day should print it out. The station printer is KRYPTON\BT1217
    • If it is not printed out when you get to the booth—print it out

To Choose and Read Stories:

  1. Select two stories that haven’t been initialed in the past couple hours
  2. For your first story, select a hard news story (not sports or entertainment)
    • If you want, your second story may be sports or entertainment, but it doesn’t have to be
  3. Read through your stories several times to make sure you have them down
    • If you aren’t 100% sure how to pronounce a word or name–look it up
    • If you still aren’t 100% sure select another story
  4. Play the KTRM News Spot in Simian
    • (Its titled “KTRM News Flash – Use at :5”)
  5. Follow the “News at :55 Template” to read your stories & weather
    • Make sure the weather has been recently refreshed
  6. Initial the stories you read with the time when you’re done
  7. DO NOT comment on the stories, read them and move on
    • Feel free to give opinions at other times during your show
